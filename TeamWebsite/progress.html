    Scrum methodology

    Sprint is a period at the end of which a product has to be delivered. Once the duration is chosen, it stays constant during the whole period of development. A new sprint starts at the end of the precedent one. We used this technique during the last 9 weeks for our experiments by setting up new goals at the beginning of each week that were to be completed before the next week so we could work on something new and continue making progress.
